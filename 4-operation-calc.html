Doodle Jump is a popular mobile/arcade arcade gam where the player controls a little four-legged character called "The Doodler." The main goal is to jump as high as possible by bouncing from platform to platform without falling. Along the way, players must avoid obstacles like monsters, black holes, and broken platforms, while collecting power-ups to help them reach higher scores. The gam features simple one-touch controls making it very fun to play.

Support: The game only supports computer or devices with keyboard controls
