Basketball Stars is a fast-paced, multiplayer basketball game developed by Miniclip, where players compete in one-on-one matches or shooting races. Featuring 3D graphics and intuitive controls, the game allows users to dribble, shoot, block, and steal in real-time against opponents from around the world. Players can customize their characters with different outfits and unlock various courts and gear as they progress. With multiple game modes and skill-based gameplay, Basketball Stars combines arcade-style action with competitive sports elements, offering both casual fun and challenging competition.

Support: The game only supports computer or devices with keyboard controls
